This is the V1 half of the V1/LO double dissociation. The prediction is asymmetric in epistemic strength: a significant cross-category drop is straightforwardly tested via paired t-test on subject-wise accuracies; preserved cross-shape decoding is inferred from the absence of a comparable drop, which is a weaker inferential move. The paper's empirical claim (`v1-category-decoding-drops-in-feedback`) reports the category drop directly; shape preservation is asserted but not separately tested in the same statistical form.
