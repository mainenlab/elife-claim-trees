The prediction concerns the temporal dynamics of orthogonality — under the orthogonal-subspace hypothesis, as the choice axis emerges in the trial leading up to choice, the 5-HT axis should remain in the orthogonal complement. The temporal-growth aspect is what makes the prediction specific (a constant orthogonality at all timepoints would be consistent with no choice axis at all).
