Carries over from v1 unchanged. The 3-D PCA projection captures whatever variance is in the top three components but discards higher-dimensional structure; whether orthogonality holds in higher dimensions is not tested. Under R1's framing, the methodological claim is no longer the source of the central finding — the GLM analysis (which does not use the super-session manifold) is the new central analysis. But the manifold analysis remains the source of the geometric corroboration of additivity, and its methodological caveats still apply to the orthogonality results that derive from it.
