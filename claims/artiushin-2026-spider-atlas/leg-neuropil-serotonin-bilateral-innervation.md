The paper contrasts this 5-HT pattern explicitly against TDC2 (which shows anterior continuous diffuse vs posterior sparse puncta; Fig2Bii) and TH (which uniformly fills each LN with a mesh; Fig2D). The 5-HT bilateral compartmentation is thus a specific, discriminable finding — not a generic statement that 5-HT is present.
