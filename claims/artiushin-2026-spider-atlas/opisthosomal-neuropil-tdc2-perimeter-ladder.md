The TDC2 perimeter tract pattern in the opisthosomal neuropil parallels ChAT and proctolin innervation of the same perimeter (Fig3B,Ciii,iv), and the TH signal (Fig3D). The inner ladder-like medial structure (crossing, ring, decussation) is most clearly expressed via TDC2 and TH; proctolin additionally reveals a midline-crossing element. This multi-transmitter convergence on shared structural motifs strengthens the observational claim.
