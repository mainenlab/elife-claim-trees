The absence of AstA and CCAP in the pedipalpal neuropil is stated as a null result ("Little to no appreciable immunoreactivity"), and is worth capturing as it differentiates the PdN neurochemically from adjacent structures such as the cheliceral neuropil. The paper notes that proctolin signal is present in an immediately adjacent medial region supplied by anterior proctolin+ somata (Fig4Biv arrow), reinforcing the sharp chemical boundary of the PdN.
