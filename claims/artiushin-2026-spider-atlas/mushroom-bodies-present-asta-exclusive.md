The presence of complete mushroom bodies in U. diversus stands in contrast to several reports of absent or simplified MBs in web-building spiders (Hanström 1928, Long 2016, 2021, Steinhoff et al. 2024), though exceptions have been noted. The AstA exclusivity among tested transmitters contrasts with C. salei literature showing anti-GAD and anti-proctolin immunoreactivity in mushroom bodies (Becherer and Schmid, 1999). The "surprise" framing in the Results text is unusually direct for a descriptive atlas paper, reflecting a genuine expectation violation — which justifies capturing this as a discrete claim rather than folding it into the general atlas scope.
