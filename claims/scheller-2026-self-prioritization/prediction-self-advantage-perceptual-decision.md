This prediction is the paper's diagnostic test for automaticity. The TOJ task with a
perceptual-decision dimension strips away any task-imposed reason to evaluate social
identity — if the self-advantage nonetheless emerges, the underlying mechanism cannot
require explicit social decoding. Tested by
`self-prioritization-perceptual-decision-automatic` (1.5 Hz self-advantage in the
perceptual-decision condition of Experiment 1) and supported by
`processing-capacity-rises-perceptual-self` (the capacity component of the rate change).
