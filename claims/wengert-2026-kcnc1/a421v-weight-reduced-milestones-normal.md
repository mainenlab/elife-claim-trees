The weight reduction is significant at all time points measured (P7, P14, P21) and mirrors
findings from Kcnc1 knockout mice (Ho et al., 1997). The preserved developmental milestones
indicate the early phenotype is predominantly metabolic rather than gross neurological, which
is consistent with the progressive nature of the inhibitory circuit dysfunction found later.
